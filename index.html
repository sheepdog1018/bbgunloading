BB Guns Quick Reference

Legends M3 Grease Gun

  1. Pull down and remove the 30‑round magazine.
  2. Unscrew CO₂ cap; insert 12 g capsule; tighten.
  3. Load up to 30 BBs; reinsert magazine.
  4. Pull charging handle to chamber.
  5. Hold trigger for full‑auto fire.

Umarex Steel‑Strike

  1. Remove magazine.
  2. Unscrew CO₂ cap; insert 12 g capsule; tighten.
  3. Open BB hatch; load six BBs; close hatch.
  4. Reinsert magazine; rack charging handle to index.
  5. Pull trigger for 6‑shot burst.

H&K MP5K‑PDW

  1. Remove 40‑round magazine.
  2. Unscrew CO₂ cap; insert 12 g capsule; tighten.
  3. Load up to 40 BBs; reinsert magazine.
  4. Pull charging handle to chamber.
  5. Pull trigger per shot (semi‑auto).

SIG P229

  1. Remove grip sleeve; unscrew CO₂ cap; insert 12 g capsule; tighten.
  2. Slide barrel catch to open breech.
  3. Load one .177 pellet nose‑first; close breech.
  4. Slap slide rearward to chamber.
  5. Pull trigger for single shot.

Crosman PFAM9B

  1. Remove 20‑round magazine; unscrew CO₂ cap; insert 12 g capsule; tighten.
  2. Load up to 20 BBs; reinsert magazine.
  3. Rack slide fully to chamber and cock.
  4. Hold trigger for full‑auto fire.

Umarex Glock 19

  1. Remove magazine; unscrew CO₂ cap; insert 12 g capsule; tighten.
  2. Load up to 19 BBs; reinsert magazine.
  3. Rack slide fully to chamber.
  4. Pull trigger per shot (semi‑auto).

Umarex Colt Peacemaker

  1. Disable Safety switch under trigger guard.
  2. Cock hammer 1/2 way.
  3. Remove shell casings; remove grip and unscrew CO₂ cap on bottom; insert 12 g capsule; tighten.
  4. Load up to 6 Pelletss; reinsert filled shells.
  5. Cock hammer fully.
  6. Pull trigger per shot (single-action).
  7. You may fill pellets w/o removing shell casings.
